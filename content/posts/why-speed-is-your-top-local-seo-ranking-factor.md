In local search engine optimization, website speed is no longer just a luxury—it is the foundational gatekeeper of your conversion rates and organic visibility. 

When a potential customer searches for services in their local area, they expect immediate answers. If your website takes more than 3 seconds to load on mobile, over **53% of visits are abandoned**.

### The Core Web Vitals Impact
Google uses Core Web Vitals as a direct search ranking signal. The three primary metrics you must optimize are:
1. **Largest Contentful Paint (LCP):** Measures loading performance. Aim for 2.5 seconds or faster.
2. **Interaction to Next Paint (INP):** Measures interface responsiveness. Aim for 200 milliseconds or less.
3. **Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS):** Measures visual stability. Aim for a score of 0.1 or less.
