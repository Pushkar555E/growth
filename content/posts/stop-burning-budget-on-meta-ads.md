Many business owners run Meta (Facebook/Instagram) ads by simply clicking the "Boost Post" button. This is the fastest way to burn your marketing budget.

Boosting a post tells Meta to optimize for **engagement** (likes, comments, clicks). But clicks do not pay the bills—leads and conversions do.

### The Conversion API Advantage
Since Apple's iOS 14 update, standard browser-based pixel tracking is highly restricted. To accurately track leads and attribute sales, you must deploy server-side tracking:

- **Meta Conversions API (CAPI):** Sends event data directly from your website's server to Meta's servers, bypassing browser ad-blockers and privacy settings.
- **Custom Event Triggers:** Set up tracking specifically when a user submits your WhatsApp booking form or completes a Free Growth Audit request.
- **Audience Mapping:** Feed conversion data back into Meta's algorithmic bidding system. This allows the ad delivery engine to find users who look exactly like your converting customers.
